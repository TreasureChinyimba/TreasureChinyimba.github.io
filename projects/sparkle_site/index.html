How We Work

STEP ONE

We begin by creating a friendly, welcoming atmosphere to help ease any anxiety your child may have. We conduct an initial assessment of your child's oral health, asking about any concerns or issues your child might have.

STEP TWO

We performs a thorough examination of your child's teeth, gums, and overall oral health. This may include checking for cavities, assessing bite alignment, and monitoring the development of permanent teeth.

STEP THREE

To conclude the visit on a positive note, We provide positive reinforcement to your child for good oral hygiene practices. As a special gesture, We present the child with a colorful and personalized certificate for being a "Super Smiler" or a "Cavity Fighter.
